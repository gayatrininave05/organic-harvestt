About Organic Harvest

Our Journey Since 2010

Founded in 2010, Organic Harvest began as a small family farm with a simple mission: to provide fresh, organic produce to our local community. What started as a modest operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in organic farming.

Today, we work with over 200 local farmers who share our commitment to sustainable agriculture and ethical farming practices. Our products reach thousands of families across the country, helping them make healthier food choices.

Our Mission

To make organic food accessible to everyone while promoting sustainable farming practices that protect our planet for future generations.

Our Vision

To create a world where organic farming is the norm, not the exception, and where every family can enjoy chemical-free, nutritious food.

Our Core Values

  • Sustainability: We practice farming methods that enrich the soil and protect ecosystems
  • Transparency: We're open about our farming practices and supply chain
  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products
  • Community: We support local farmers and give back to our community

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Organic Harvest different from other organic brands?

Organic Harvest stands out through our farmer-first approach, rigorous quality standards, and commitment to sustainability beyond just certification requirements. We invest in our partner farmers through education and fair pricing, ensuring long-term relationships that benefit everyone in our supply chain.

How do you ensure your products are truly organic?

We maintain USDA Organic certification through annual audits, surprise inspections, and rigorous documentation. Our farms use only approved organic inputs, and we conduct regular soil and product testing. Additionally, we've implemented blockchain technology to provide full traceability from farm to table.

Where can I buy Organic Harvest products?

Our products are available in over 1,500 stores nationwide, through our online store with home delivery, and at farmers' markets in 12 states. Use our store locator to find the nearest retailer carrying our products.

Do you offer farm tours or educational programs?

Yes! We offer seasonal farm tours, school field trips, and weekend workshops on organic farming techniques. Our flagship farm in Vermont also hosts a summer internship program for aspiring organic farmers. Check our events calendar for upcoming opportunities.
